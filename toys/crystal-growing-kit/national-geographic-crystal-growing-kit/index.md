# National Geographic Crystal Growing Kit

Grow 3 vibrant crystals in different colors with real crystal specimens included. Features learning guide explaining crystal formation and geology concepts.

## Why it scores well
- **Repeatability (3)**: Limited by materials (3 crystals per kit), but the magic of growing crystals makes kids want to do it again
- **Session length (3)**: Active time is 15-20 minutes setup, then passive waiting for crystal growth over days, then 10 minutes observation
- **Cleanup (4)**: Most materials disposable after use, crystals can be displayed, minimal ongoing storage needs
