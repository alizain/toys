# National Geographic Rock Collection Starter Kit

Collection of 15+ rock, mineral, and fossil specimens with identification guide and display case. Includes magnifying glass for close examination.

## Why it scores well
- **Repeatability (4)**: Kids repeatedly examine specimens, show to friends, and start their own collections inspired by the kit
- **Session length (3)**: Initial exploration 20-30 minutes, then shorter 5-10 minute sessions revisiting favorites
- **Cleanup (5)**: Specimens store neatly in divided display case, sits on shelf beautifully
